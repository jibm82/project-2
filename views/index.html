¿WHAT IS BLOODMAP?

Bloodmap is a country-wide database where patients looking for blood and blood donating campaigns can connect with our registered blood donors.

¿HOW DOES IS IT WORK?

Active blood donors have to register and create a profile. Once that is done, you will be granted access to a map where donation campaigns and patients are currently looking for blood.

A blood donation campaign can be set up by simply adding up the patient/campaign information. Considering the location of the campaign, an alert with the details will be sent to the active users.
